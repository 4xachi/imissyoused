Fun games with a meaningful twist. Learn the rules!

SPIN-TASTIC MACHINE

The SPIN-TASTIC MACHINE (Human Slot Machine) is an engaging activity where advocates act as the moving reels of a slot machine. When the lever is pulled, they reveal random icons with trivia, tips, challenges, or prizes inside, creating excitement and memorable moments for everyone

STRIKE A POSE

Participants will strike a pose, and they will receive a personalized doodle of themselves. These doodles serve as meaningful keepsakes, reminding them of their fun and memorable experience.
